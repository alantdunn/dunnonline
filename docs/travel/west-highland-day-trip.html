Before You Go

A few things worth knowing to make sure the day runs smoothly.

🚂 Sunday Timetable
  • The West Highland Line runs approximately 3 services each way on Sundays
  • Suggested departure: ~10:41 from Helensburgh Upper
  • Always confirm on scotrail.co.uk before you travel
  • Check for engineering works — this line is subject to occasional weekend closures
⚠️ Fewer trains means fewer chances to get home — agree on a return time before you set off.
🐾 Dogs on ScotRail
  • 2 dogs travel free on ScotRail — no ticket needed
  • Dogs must be kept on leads throughout the journey
  • Dogs are not permitted on seats
  • If there is a rail replacement bus, dogs cannot board — check before travelling
⚠️ Always confirm dog policy with ScotRail when checking the timetable, especially on Sundays.
